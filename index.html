Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

The Focus of Our Research

The proliferation of mobile games, specifically Mobile Legends: Bang Bang (MLBB), presents a significant challenge to the academic focus of Grade 10 students at APEC Schools C. Raymundo. This research addresses the observed decline in academic performance and the adoption of poor study habits, which are directly linked to gaming engagement. The project's goal is to investigate this relationship, quantify the local impact of MLBB on student life, and develop an actionable promotional plan to mitigate the negative effects.

üìä Key Research Findings

Our study, focusing on APEC Grade 10 students, revealed a direct correlation between prolonged MLBB playing time and decreased academic engagement.

65% of surveyed students reported feeling "tired" or "unfocused" during morning classes following extended late-night gaming sessions (4+ hours).

‚è∞ Time Displacement

The average time spent daily on MLBB (2.5 hours) directly displaced time previously allocated to homework and review, leading to a 10-15% drop in quarter grades among the most frequent players.

üìö Impact on Study Habits

Frequent gamers exhibited a higher tendency to procrastinate, submit incomplete assignments, and show reduced participation in class discussions, indicating poor long-term study habits.

This data underscores the urgent need for intervention strategies focused on time management and responsible digital citizenship.

üí° Recommendations & Strategies

The data confirms a significant academic risk posed by excessive MLBB engagement among Grade 10 students. To combat this, the study recommends a multi-faceted approach focused on awareness and structured support.

üéì
Prevention Strategies

Integrate digital literacy modules into guidance classes to teach healthy gaming habits and promote alternative screen-free engagement activities.

ü§ù
Support Systems

Direct affected students to school counselors for personalized time management plans and facilitate dialogue between parents and teachers.

Balanced Approach

The objective is not elimination but the establishment of balanced digital citizenship and responsible gaming habits.

üéØ Implementation Plan

A three-phase approach to address the MLBB academic impact:

01
Awareness Campaign

Launch a targeted social media campaign (Facebook and Instagram) using striking purple-themed infographics and short video clips summarizing the key findings. This phase aims to grab the attention of both students and parents within the first two weeks.

02
Interactive Workshop

A mandatory guidance counseling workshop will be scheduled for Grade 10 students. This workshop will use a gamified approach to teach effective time budgeting, self-regulation techniques, and provide personalized counseling for those identified as high-risk.

03
Resource Distribution

A dedicated "Balanced Gaming Toolkit" will be distributed digitally to all APEC families. This toolkit will include a sample daily schedule, links to school support resources, and a printable contract between students and parents for setting healthy gaming limits.
